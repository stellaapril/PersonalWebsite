DESIGN4U ONLINE DESIGN MARKET SYSTEM

JAVA | XHTML | SQL

SUMMARY

Project Overview:

The Design4U Online Design Market is an online design trading platform that provides a market for designer and design services for customers with design needs. It used customer to customer (c2c) business and business to consumer (B2B) mode. Designers can build their own studio on the platform and publishes its own designs for demonstrating their design capability and style to attract customers. Customers can choose the studio for their own design needs. Both parties shall negotiate the content, price and deadline of the design project online. The Online Design Market is responsible for safeguarding the common interests of both parties and providing secure trading methods.

Purpose and Scope of this Specification:

With the development of the society, design has become more and more important in our lives, and it is reflected in all aspects. Traditional design companies are more for large projects to provide design and design costs high. However, the life of the increasing demand for design from small projects, such as one’s home backyard, a local restaurant website, or a personal logo design…etc. From the user's point of view, they lack a convenient, quick and affordable way to find the right designer. At that same time, from the designers’ point of view, many talented designers lack a platform for users to find themselves. Therefore, this project serves as a bridge, making the design simpler and more convenient, and making the idea more practical. The scope is global, and the goal is to have millions of active users.

PERFORMANCE REQUIREMENTS

The number of terminals to be supported is no less than 1000 for online design market system.

The number of simultaneous users to be supported is no less than 1000 for the online design market system.

The online design market should handle sign up information, orders request, live chat, design project upload and download.

95% of the payment transaction shall be processed in less than 1 second.

95% of the designer accept order transaction shall be processed in less than 3 minutes.

95% of the live chat shall be processed in less than 5 second.

The number of users sign up and place order is no less than 3000 for normal workload conditions of online design market system.

The number of users sign up and place order is no less than 5000 for the peak workload conditions of online design market system.
