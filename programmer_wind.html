Self-Form-Finding Wind Tunnel to Environmental-Performance Building Design

ARDUINO | RHINO

2018 The Association for Computer-Aided Architectural Design Research in Asia International Conference
Tsinghua Digital Design Workshop
Advisor: Philip Yuan, Jiawei Yao, Yuqiong Lin

The change of the building morphology directly affects the surrounding environment, while the evaluation of these environment data becomes the main basis for the genetic iterations of the building morphology. Thus, the architect is suggested to take the wind environment as the main morphology generation factor in the early stage of the building design. Wind Tunnel Visualization Workshop uses Arduino to carry out the dynamic design of the building model, which can not only connect the sensor to monitor the wind environment data, but also contribute the building model to correlate with the wind environment data in real time.

--Feng Yuan,Jiawei Yao,Yuqiong Lin,Jingyuan Zheng
